Submit an encoding proposal

Please fill in the fields below and press the Submit button to add your encoding to the database as a proposal. See the description of the process to find out what will happen after you made your submission.

Notes:

  • The ID may only contain letters, numbers, underscore and hyphen. You should use capital letters. Encoding IDs which only differ by their case are considered duplicates.
  • The short description provides a long (technical) name for the encoding. Do not include specification details.
  • Contact is the person or company who submits the encoding to the database. It is not necessarily the creator of the encoding format.
  • The comment field should contain additional specifications how to use the encoding, like e.g. how the fields of AudioFormat are interpreted. Do not include technical description how to implement FormatConversionProviders. You cannot include html code, but complete http and ftp URL's and email adresses are linked automatically.
  • After pressing the Submit button, you'll have the possibility to verify your proposal. Further changes can be requested by sending an email to Florian or Matthias.
  • With pressing the Submit button you agree that your provided information is made publicly available.
